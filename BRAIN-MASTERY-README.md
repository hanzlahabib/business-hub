## üìã Daily Routine Summary (Quick Reference)

### Morning (First 90 min of day)
1. **Fajr Prayer** (15 min)
   - Pray on time
   - Read 1-2 pages Quran with reflection
   - Make dua for guidance

2. **3 M's Routine** (15-30 min)
   - Movement: Exercise/yoga
   - Mindset: Affirmations/intentions
   - Mindfulness: Meditation/breathwork

3. **Brain Dump Journaling** (20-30 min)
   - Stream of consciousness writing
   - No judgment, just flow

### During Day (Peak Energy Hours)
4. **One High-Leverage Action** (1-2 hours)
   - The ONE thing that moves you toward your main goal
   - Do it when you have most energy

### Evening (Before Bed)
5. **Gratitude to Allah** (5-10 min)
   - List 10 things: "Alhamdulillah for..."

6. **Evening Reflection** (5-10 min)
   - What went well?
   - What did I learn?
   - How did I show up as future self?

7. **No Cheap Dopamine After 8 PM**
   - No scrolling, binge watching, late snacking

8. **Quality Sleep** (7-9 hours)
   - Dark room, cool temp
   - Pray Isha + Ayat al-Kursi
   - In bed by 10 PM

## üí° Key Concepts to Remember

### Neuroplasticity
Your brain can change at ANY age. You're not stuck with who you were. You're becoming who you choose to be.

### Identity Shift
You must BECOME the person first (in your mind), then DO the actions. Not the other way around.

### The 3 Procrastination Reasons
1. **Identity Mismatch** - Choose new identity NOW
2. **Subconscious Fear** - Label it specifically, reframe it
3. **Cheap Dopamine** - Withhold rewards until after big task

### Manifestation Formula
1. Identify the FEELING you want
2. List reasons you already have to feel that way
3. List activities YOU can do to create that feeling
Result: You're a match. Now take aligned action.

### Islamic Integration
- This entire journey is ibadah (worship)
- You're studying Allah's creation (your brain)
- Tawakkul = aligned action + trust in Allah's timing
- Gratitude (shukr) activates blessings neuroscience + spirituality

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Q: What if I miss a day?
**A:** Give yourself grace. Missing one day doesn't ruin 90. Learn the lesson, get back on track immediately. The old you beats yourself up. The new you gives grace and keeps moving.

### Q: What if I don't have 2-3 hours for all these practices?
**A:** Lower the bar. Do 5 minutes of each. Something beats nothing. Build momentum first, intensity later. Even 30 minutes total daily will transform you.

### Q: What if I'm not seeing results?
**A:** Trust the process. Compound effect takes time. Keep your part: aligned action + becoming a match. Allah's part: timing + delivery. Detach from timeline.

### Q: Can I modify this plan?
**A:** Absolutely! These are guidelines, not rules. Adapt to YOUR life, YOUR schedule, YOUR needs. The principles stay, the execution can flex.

### Q: What if my family doesn't understand?
**A:** "Being misunderstood is the tax of being authentic." Love them, but don't let them stop you. Find YOUR people who get it.

### Q: Do I need to be Muslim to do this?
**A:** The neuroscience works for everyone. But I've integrated Islamic principles because that's how I understand the world. Adapt to your faith tradition if different.

### Q: What after 90 days?
**A:** This is just the beginning! 90 days builds foundation. Then you design your next 90-day sprint. Growth is lifelong. This is a lifestyle.
